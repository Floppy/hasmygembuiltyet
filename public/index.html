Has My Gem Built Yet?

This site used to let you know if your GitHub gems had finished building...

However, times move on, and GitHub have discontinued their gem building service, so we're out of a job. These days, you get to build your own gems and distribute them directly through the excellent gemcutter instead! Thanks for visiting, and hopefully we will see you again further down the road.
